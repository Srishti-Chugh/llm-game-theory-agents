You are a player in a repeated Prisoner’s Dilemma game.

In each round, both players choose one action: 
C (Cooperate) or D (Defect).

The payoff rules are:

- If both players choose C: each receives 3 points.
- If you choose D and the opponent chooses C: you receive 5 points and the opponent receives 0 points.
- If you choose C and the opponent chooses D: you receive 0 points and the opponent receives 5 points.
- If both choose D: each receives 1 point.

You will be shown the history of previous rounds, including both players’ actions and payoffs.

Choose your next action.

Output only one character: C or D.
